# AI Health Navigator

[![Demo Video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/2hDE-_BSOEA/0.jpg)](https://youtu.be/2hDE-_BSOEA)

AI Health Navigator is an **AI-powered health tracking and wellness assistant** that interprets user-reported symptoms and sentiments to provide meaningful insights, suggestions, and recommendations for healthier living.

This project won **2nd Position at a GDG Hackathon** for its innovative use of AI and natural language processing in healthcare.

---

##  Features

-  **Sentiment and Symptom Analysis**  
  Users enter how they feel in natural language, and the system analyzes the sentiment and extracts health-related cues.

-**Health Tracking**  
  Logs user inputs over time to understand trends in mood, symptoms, and wellness.

-  **Personalized Health Suggestions**  
  Based on sentiment and symptom analysis, the app suggests relevant health advice, lifestyle tips, and preventive measures.

-  **Interactive User Experience**  
  Simple text-based input for intuitive interaction, enabling accessibility for all users.
